How We Can Help

IAM.Inc provides expertise in the following areas:

  • Delivery and implementation of SAP Advanced Performance Management (APM) and Business Network Asset Collaboration (BNAC).
  • Experience with earlier versions of SAP IAM products (Asset Central Foundation, Asset Intelligence Network, Asset Strategy and Performance Management, Predictive Maintenance and Service).
  • Deep knowledge of other asset performance tools (Meridium, GE-APM, Infor, ABB, Schneider, Siemens, IBM Maximo).
  • Expertise in IoT, AI, machine learning, and data analytics to optimize asset performance and maintenance.
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) with proven record of increased overall equipment effectiveness and reduced maintenance cost.
  • Specialized partnerships for SAP Mobile Applications (SAP Asset Manager).
  • Innovative pricing models including lean rates and outcome-based pricing tied to improvements in asset performance metrics.

We offer a collaborative approach, working closely with maintenance and IT teams to ensure smooth project execution. Our proven documentation approach ensures effective knowledge transfer, helping clients build capabilities in SAP IAM.
